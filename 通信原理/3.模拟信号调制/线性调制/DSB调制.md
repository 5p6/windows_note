### 双边带调制
调制后的信号为
$$\begin{align}
    s_{DSB}  = m(t) \cos(2 \pi f_c t)
\end{align}$$

其频谱为
$$\begin{align}
    S_{DSB}(f) = \frac{1}{2}[M(f  + f_c) + M(f - f_c)]
\end{align}$$

设 $m(t)$ 的功率谱为 $P_m(f)$
$$\begin{align}
    P_{DSB}(f) = \frac{1}{4}[P_m(f + f_c) + P_m(f - f_c)]  
\end{align}$$

功率
$$\begin{align}
    P_{DSB} = \frac{1}{2}\bar{m^2(t)}
\end{align}$$

信号带宽
$$\begin{align}
    B_{DSB} = 2f_H
\end{align}$$
